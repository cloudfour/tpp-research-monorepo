## Details Lockup

The details lockup is a structural component used for page layouts. It organizes
content into three slots: `header`, `content`, and `footer`. On small screens
they are stacked vertically in that order with a gap between them. On large
screens the `content` slot is positioned to the left and has height equal to the
`header` and `footer` slots combined.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Basic">
    {() => {
      return html`
        <c4-details-lockup>
          <div slot="header">
            <div style="background: #eee;">Header</div>
          </div>
          <div slot="content">
            <div style="background: #eee;">Content</div>
          </div>
          <div slot="footer">
            <div style="background: #eee;">Footer</div>
          </div>
        </c4-details-lockup>
      `;
    }}
  </Story>
</Preview>
